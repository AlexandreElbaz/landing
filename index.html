Ideas

Fly to New Zealand if you're looking for adventure

You can also go to Choquequirao in Peru if you want to be an adventurer again.

If you just want a family vacation, San Francisco Crosstown Trail in California will be perfect for you!

If you also want a family trip, go to the United Kingdom to Manchester, where you can do lots of family activities!

If you want to spend a vacation surrounded by nature, you can go to Big Bend National Park in Texas

If you also want to see greenery and be in nature, I advise you to go to Botswana in Central Africa. This is where you will find your happiness!

If you want to be in a community to discover a new country, I strongly advise you to go to Alberta in Canada

Another dream destination for community living is to go to Milwaukee in Wisconsin where you will not be disappointed.

If you want to visit a country full of culture, Egypt will be your best choice! Indeed, Egypt is a country that is full of history, traditions

Another travel idea that will allow you to return with even more personal culture, I strongly advise you to go to Busan in South Korea.
